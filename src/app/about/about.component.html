HEY THERE!

We are the SinfonIA team

We are the robotics and AI research group at Universidad de Los Andes, a team passionate about merging technology and human interaction. We currently work in the following subgroups: Perception, Navigation, Manipulation and Speech, each of which refines a piece of the social interaction pie. Likewise, members in Interface and Toolkit ensure that our robots are top-notch, while task members orchestrate the grand symphony of robotic tasks. We are a multidisciplinary dream team, merging engineering prowess with the creative minds of several university departments. Join us on this journey where technology and humanity come together to shape the future of robotics.

Our vision

We aspire to forge a future where technology and research merge into a perfect symphony. Our vision encompasses leadership in orchestrating innovation in emerging fields, fostering collaboration that resonates with diversity and creativity. We seek to impact society significantly, transcending beyond research to improve lives and understand our world. We strive to stand out as leaders in scientific harmonization, where each member contributes to a collective composition of excellence and originality. We envision the construction of a constant technological crescendo, taking our research to new heights and ensuring a lasting impact on society, convinced that true innovation arises from the perfect union of creative minds and transformative technology.

Our timeline

2018

SinfonIA Foundation

This year marked the birth of SinfonIA, an exciting student initiative driven by its pioneers. The genesis of SinfonIA was driven by the arrival of a fascinating pepper robot named Opera, donated by Bancolombia in the SinfonIA alliance. This alliance, formed by the Universidad de los Andes, Universidad Santo Tom√°s and Universidad de Magdalena, is a collaboration between industry and academia to support the application and development of artificial intelligence and robotics in research projects.

2019

Robocup 2019

Our team participated for the first time in the RoboCup competition in Sydney, achieving a respectable fourth place. Despite being a novel experience, we consider it a significant achievement that leaves us with valuable lessons for future occasions.

2020

We had a new robot member!

Our group has obtained a new member called Nova, a Pepper robot acquired entirely by the Universidad de Los Andes. Nova's arrival had a positive impact on several aspects, from improving our daily work to enhancing our research. In addition, he has brought his spark to the events in which we participate.

2022

Robocup 2022

We were honored to participate in the RoboCup competition in Bangkok, Thailand, where we demonstrated our commitment and innovation in the field of robotics. Here our team achieved an outstanding second place, standing out in this challenging scenario and reaffirming our commitment to excellence in the competition.

2023

Robocup 2023

In our most recent foray into the RoboCup competition, this time in Bordeaux, France, we celebrated another outstanding achievement by securing second place. This competition adds to our previous experiences, such as in Sydney and Bangkok. These successes underscore our continued commitment and our constant innovation in the field of robotics. We are excited about what the future holds and look forward to continuing to raise our standards with each new challenge.
